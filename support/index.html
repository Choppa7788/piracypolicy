Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my premium subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Make sure you're logged into the same Apple ID or Google account you used to purchase the subscription.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! Once you've downloaded the app, you can browse all cocktail recipes offline. However, some features like syncing favorites require an internet connection.
How do I change the app language?
Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred language. The app supports multiple languages including English, Japanese, and more.
Why can't I access premium features?
Make sure your subscription is active and try restoring purchases. If the issue persists, contact our support team with your purchase receipt.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account. Go to your account settings and manage subscriptions from there.
Is the app safe for users under 21?
The app is designed for users of legal drinking age. While recipes are educational, we strongly encourage responsible use and compliance with local laws.
